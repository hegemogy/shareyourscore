About This Project

ShareYourScore is a mission‑driven data science initiative focused on understanding Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs) in Southern Indiana and the Louisville region. This area has one of the highest rates of children with incarcerated parents in the nation, and ACE scores help illuminate the factors surrounding this crisis.

This early version of the site is a simple placeholder while development continues. The long‑term goal is to support community partners, nonprofits, and local leaders working to improve stability, healing, and opportunity for children and families.

Status

This project is currently in private development. Features, data tools, and community‑facing components will appear here as they are built.
